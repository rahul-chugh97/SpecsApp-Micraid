Tool for Blind

Without vision it is challenging for a visually impaired to access internet, read PDF, handwritten documents or websites in their mother tongue. Especially, the visually impaired people in India find it difficult to access common things that normal people can easily access because of language problems.

Read in Hindi

This will help 15 million visually impaired people in India to read PDF and handwritten documents in their native language. The Application will convert PDF documents written in native languages to speech supporting visually impaired by helping them understand the documents in languages like Hindi, Marathi or Bengali.
